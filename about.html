About Kitso-Botlhale Development Services

Empowering communities since 2007 with passion, purpose, and proven results

Our Story

Kitso-Botlhale Development Services was established as a vision in 2004 and delivered in 2007 by a young black woman with a passion for community empowerment and social justice.

Our journey began with a simple yet powerful belief: that every individual and every community has the potential for greatness. All they need is the right support, tools, and opportunities to unlock that potential.

From humble beginnings in Soweto, we have grown into a comprehensive development organization that touches lives through multiple channels - from our award-winning UniQue cleaning products to our transformational workshops and training programs.
